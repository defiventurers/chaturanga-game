Rules Snapshot

Core movement, dice outcomes, turn flow, promotion, elimination, and victory remain unchanged.

Dice MappingExact Variant
  • 1 or 5: Pawn or King
  • 2: Elephant
  • 3: Horse
  • 4 or 6: Ship
Variant Notesv2 Behavior
  • Only legal moves count. No fake playable turns.
  • If a piece matches both dice, you choose which die to consume.
  • Promotion follows the destination-square mapping for this variant.
  • Promotion may create extra kings, but only the starting king is royal.
  • A team is eliminated only when it has no kings left on the board.
  • Eliminated players are skipped automatically.
  • If the unused die has no legal move left, the turn auto-ends.
  • Remaining pieces of an eliminated player are removed immediately.
