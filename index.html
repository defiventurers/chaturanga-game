How To Play

Quick Rules & Turn Flow

Learn the existing ruleset in arctic style. Mechanics remain unchanged.

1. Roll Dice

Each turn starts with two dice. Dice values unlock piece types: 1 or 5 = Snow Guard or Frost King, 2 = War Mammoth, 3 = Aurora Unicorn, 4 or 6 = Icebreaker.

2. Select a Piece

Pick one of your own pieces that matches an unused die and has at least one legal move. If both dice apply, you choose which die to spend.

3. Move or Capture

Green and Blue snow guards move vertically. Red and Pink snow guards move horizontally. Captures follow the current live rules only—nothing extra is added.

4. Promotion

When a pawn reaches a promotion destination square, it may promote according to the existing destination mapping. Promotion can create non-royal kings.

5. Elimination & Victory

A team is eliminated only when it has no kings left on the board. When only one live team remains, that team wins the game.

6. Bots

If fewer than four humans are selected, missing colors become bots. Bots roll the same dice, use the same legal move engine, and never cheat.
